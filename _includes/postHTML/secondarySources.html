What are secondary sources?

Secondary sources address or analyze events, people, words, or topics.

  • Provides background information, analysis, and unique perspectives.
  • Check the bibliography for leads to primary and more secondary sources

Examples of secondary sources...

  • Newspaper articles
  • Journal and magazine articles
  • Books
